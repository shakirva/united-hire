Due to the high demand for suitable nursing staff in Germany, well-trained foreign nursing professionals have good chances of finding a job on the German labor market. However, this process often turns out to be difficult, requiring appropriate support for foreign nurses. As United Hire, we organize this process so that the foreign nurse would be entitled to support and be accompanied at all times. We advise, qualify and place foreign nursing staff comprehensively.

Steps of the recruitment process

Consulting and Candidate Search

In a consultation, you will be informed about the entire process that is necessary to be able to hire a Filipino skilled worker. Your individual needs will be addressed and the matching process for finding a suitable candidate will be initiated.

Assessment Test and Language Courses

For the selection of our candidates, interviews are conducted in which the professional and personal suitability is clarified. In addition, we conduct an assessment test to determine motivation and suitability.

Video Calls / Monitoring / Development Status

You can view the application documents of the healthcare professional and get to know her or him in an interview via video call.

Entry & Visa

All measures necessary to enable the health professional to enter and access the German labor market are undertaken.

Recognition

The preparation for the knowledge test already begins in the homeland and the nurses are prepared for their work in Germany in our training center.

Commencement of Employment

The start of the activity can take place upon consultation directly after the entry or after conclusion of the knowledge examination.
